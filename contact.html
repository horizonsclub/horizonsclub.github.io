We want to hear your voice! Horizons values the curiosity that Punahou students have. As such, we want anyone joining to be as inclusive a process as possible. Writers, copy editors, and illustrators wanting to begin producing content for us can do so almost immediately. We have training resources, processes, and staff that can help individuals starting at any skill level gain the confidence, skill, and experience needed to produce high-level work.

If you’re interested in becoming a part of Horizons, please reach out to horizonsclub1@gmail.com. From there, someone will help get you started!
